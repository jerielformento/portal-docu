Open Jobs Model

This object is limited to Open Jobs Model. Open Job is limited only for Lead Project Managers (LPM) in the USA office. It's a summary monitoring status of their Jobs. The LPMs fill out the open Job form weekly. It's intended for all database activities of Manpower such as SELECT, UPDATE, DELETE and INSERT.
