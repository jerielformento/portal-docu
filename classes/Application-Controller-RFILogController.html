Rules for this report (DELINQUENT RFI)

  • RFI over 7 days from "date sent" and no "date ansd"
  • Availability: Accessible to all management
      - The trigger would be
    • if the date in the "date sent" column is 7 days or more
    • and nothing is in the "date ansd" column,
    • and "Closed" column is not Yes,
    • and IDS RFI No. is not emmpty
      • then it will show up on this report. This needs to be sortable by PPM, LPM, and Job#/call letters.
