RORating Model - Remote Office Rating Model

This object is limited to Remote Office Rating activities. Rating is only available to the Lead Project Manager (LPM) and Quality Assurance (QA) Team. The summary is visible only to the management. It's intended for all database activities of Remote Office Rating such as SELECT, UPDATE, DELETE and INSERT.
