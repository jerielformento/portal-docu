In-House Hour Monitoring Model - THIS METHOD IS HIGHLY CONDENTIAL

This object is for In-House Hour Monitoring Controller, it's a collection of Estimated Hours for CD Hours, Engineering Hours, QR Hours, QAQC Hours, Core Hours, Drafting Support Hours, and Bid Jobs. This is strictly confidential and uses only by the President of IDS (Corby Tolentino). Other portions are available to Engineering Team Lead for viewing only.

It's intended for all database activities of Employee 201 File such as SELECT, UPDATE, DELETE and INSERT.
