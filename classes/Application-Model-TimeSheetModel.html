TimeSheet Model

This object is limited to TimeSheet activities such as the following

  • My Timesheet -  available to all in-house employee (Philippines, India, and USA office) 
  • All TImesheet - available to HR/ Secretary
  • Team Timesheet - available  for Project Manager
  • Edit Timesheet - available to HR/Secretary
  • Timesheet & Under Time Monitoring - available to the HR department
  • TimeSheet Summary (PHP/IND)- available to the HR department in PH & India
  • TimeSheet Summary (USA) - available to USA employee (c/o Laura)
  • Work Schedule Official Phillippine Office - available to the HR department
  • Work Scheduled Official USA Office - available to the HR Department
  • Total Hours Summary - available to the HR Department
  • Delinquent Time Sheet for the Philippines office - available to the HR department
  • Delinquent Time Sheet for the USA office - available to USA management (Laura, Mischelle, Camil)
  • Activate Time Sheet for Administrator - available only to Dolly and Jackie

It's intended for all database activities of Timesheet such as SELECT, UPDATE, DELETE and INSERT.
