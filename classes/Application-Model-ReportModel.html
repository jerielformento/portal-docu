Report Models

This object is limited to Report Controller. It's a collection of all reports under the report menu. Such as Total Job Summary, Billing Spreadsheet, Old Billing Spreadsheet, Cost of Production, etc. It's intended for all database activities of Manpower such as SELECT, UPDATE, DELETE and INSERT.
